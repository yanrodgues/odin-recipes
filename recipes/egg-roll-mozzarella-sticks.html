Egg Roll Mozzarella Sticks

Homepage

Description

The most simple and delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • 5 egg roll wrappers
  • 10 sticks string cheese
  • water
  • oil, for frying
  • marinara sauce

Step by step

  1. Slice the egg roll wrappers in half. Place a stick of string cheese on the edge of the egg roll wrapper.
    Fold the sides of the wrapper over the ends of the cheese
  2. Wet the other edge of the wrapper with water, then roll it up tightly, pressing the edge to make sure itâ€™s sealed.
  3. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and cheese. Heat oil in a pot.
  4. Fry the sticks until golden brown, then drain on a paper towel. Serve with marinara.
